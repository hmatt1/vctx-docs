## Operator Precedence

vctx supports standard hardware operators.

### Arithmetic

+ (Add)
- (Subtract)
* (Multiply)
/ (Divide)
% (Modulus)

### Bitwise

| (OR)
^ (XOR)
& (AND)
~ (NOT / Invert)

### Logical

|| (Logical OR)
&& (Logical AND)
! (Logical NOT)

### Comparison

== (Equal)
!== (Not Equal)
< (Less Than)
> (Greater Than)
<== (Less or Equal)
>== (Greater or Equal)

### Shift

<< (Shift Left)
>> (Shift Right)

### Ternary

cond ? true_val : false_val
